## üîß How to Use the Application

Once you've installed the application, follow these steps to get started:

1. **Open the Application:**
   Locate the application on your desktop or in your applications folder. Double-click to open it.

2. **Import Your Data:**
   You can easily import your sales data. Click on the "Import" button on the main screen. Select your file (CSV or Excel format) containing sales data.

3. **Analyze Your Data:**
   Explore the available features:

   - **KPI Tracking:** View key metrics like total sales, growth rate, and more.
   - **Sales Forecasting:** Use built-in algorithms to predict future sales based on historical data.
   - **Automated Reporting:** Generate reports with a single click. Output formats include PDF and Excel.

4. **Visualize Your Data:**
   The application offers various charts and graphs to represent your data visually. Use the dashboard to customize your views.

5. **Save Your Work:**
   After analyzing, you may want to save your findings. Click "Save," and choose a location for your report.
